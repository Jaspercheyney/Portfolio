About

Hi, my name is Jasper Cheyney.

I'm an English product designer living in Brighton, England. I currently lead the Training app and Membership teams at Gymshark, helping unite the fitness community. I built my first website at the age of 12 and from that point fell in love with all aspects of design. I am passionate about crafting exceptional experiences that improve people’s lives. I have a strong appreciation for minimal, high-craft design and believe when it comes to user experience, less is more.

In my spare time I enjoy building native apps in Swift such as Instinct, which I designed, built and launched in August 2025, available on the App Store for iOS & iPadOS. I also create free design resources to help others, like this iOS Dynamic Island template, free on the Figma Community.

I'm hugely passionate about interior design and get tremendous pleasure in applying my visual style to the things around me in the real world. Outside of design, I enjoy part-time obsessive gaming, technology tinkering and bodybuilding.
