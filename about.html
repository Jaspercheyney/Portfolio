About

Hi, my name is Jasper Cheyney.

I'm a English product designer living in Brighton, England. I currently lead the brilliant design team at Pragmatic, a Brighton based digital agency. I built my first website at the age of 12 and from that point fell in love with all aspects of design. I have a strong appreciation for minimal, focused design and firmly believe when it comes to user experience, less is more.

In my spare time I enjoy working on side projects such as Slate, a design system (more to come), conceptualising bitesize user interfaces, which you can check out on my Instagram and Dribbble and building small static sites, like this one.

I'm hugely passionate about interior design and get tremendous pleasure in applying my visual style to the things around me in the real world. Outside of design, I enjoy part-time obsessive gaming, technology tinkering and bodybuilding.
