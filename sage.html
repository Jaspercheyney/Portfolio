Sage

Designing the Sage official blog

Role

Lead Designer - UX, Visual and Interaction

Team

Carmine Colicino, Tommy Ferry, Simon Champion

Awards

UK Blog Awards 2018, Going Global 2019

Creating a global content platform

Sage is largest technology company in the U.K. and needed to launch a new financially focused blog that streamlined their digital presence enabling them to generate organic leads via content. The aim was to create an industry leading content experience suitable for global markets in all languages that provided user value and generated new sources of revenue.

Design approach

I worked closely with Sage to ensure the content structure was based on their intended target audience. From this I was able to intuitively design the experience for each audience creating opportunity for more personalised, engaging content.

During the project I placed emphasis on designing a focused, distraction-free reading experience that also effectively generated leads. I applied a number of design solutions including optimal line length for reading (50-60 characters per line), accessible colour contrast (AA) and intelligent interaction design delivering lead capture throughout the user journey.

Throughout and after the project I worked side by side with our in house development team and Sage senior stakeholders to design, animate and then iterate and test numerous solutions to optimise the websites lead performance and user experience.

Design system

While collaborating with the Sage.com brand team I took their product oriented Design System and redesigned it for use in an editorial environment. This required refactoring and iterating the visual style to ensure the experience was both faithful with the brand and fit for purpose.
